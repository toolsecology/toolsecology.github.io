Meet Interactive Media

    Interactive media is an incredibly powerful marketing tool. No wonder why calculators, quizzes, and recommendation tools are quickly rising in popularity. Due to their interactivity and personalization, they address all the issues that limit blogs and eBooks to mere content consumption.

    Customers interact with your product or service which gives them value upfront. It facilitates two-way communication and starts a dialogue. Customers enter their real data aiming to find quick answers to their most pressing questions or get more insight into their personality. 

    You can show comparisons. For instance, “How Much You Save With Our Products Vs Theirs”. Or an expert system along the lines of “Which Product Is The Right Fit For My Needs?” or "Which Gifts Would You Recommend?" would serve customers quickly and efficiently. 

    Similarly, a simulator like "Customize Our Product" would introduce  customers to your products while directly addressing some of the user’s most pressing questions. 

    Imagine how much conversion an assessment like "Test Your Knowledge" would generate for an online course or how much lead generation a calculator along the lines of “Calculate Your Risk" would have for an insurance company.
